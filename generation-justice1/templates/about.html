Who We Are

A home for students who care about justice.

Generation Justice is a member-based student network for young people who want to understand social issues, build leadership skills, and create positive change in their schools and cities.

Become a Member

Our identity

We are students, mentors, organizers, writers, designers, and speakers. Members join to learn together, create media, support one another, and turn ideas into real projects.

Our values

  • Courage in public conversation
  • Respect for different experiences
  • Inclusion across schools and communities
  • Creativity in media and campaigns
  • Responsibility for shared action
