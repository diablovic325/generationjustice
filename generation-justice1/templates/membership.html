Membership Application

Apply to become a member.

The site is designed as a members-only hub. After the form is completed, the applicant is registered, logged in, and given a membership certificate with a registration number and participation level.

Participation Levels

Four levels of membership.

Level 1

Ambassador

Accepted into the programme and able to participate in online discussions, events, and competitions.

Level 2

Active Ambassador

Participates in discussions and contributes content for the monthly magazine and online books.

Level 3

Senior Ambassador

Leads initiatives and events, helps recruit new members, and supports project activity.

Level 4

Country Lead

Elite tier for members who lead activity in their country under the supervision of the Trustees.

Required Details

Application form.

This form collects the contact details needed for membership: name, address, email, phone number, country, organization, and interests.
